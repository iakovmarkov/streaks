Privacy Policy

We do not collect any personal information, besides the information needed to provide the service. Only data you explicitly send to Streaks will be stored. This includes your Telegram ID, your timezone, the content and lengths of your streaks, as well as any other message you exchange with Streaks bot.

Your data is never shared with a third party for analytics, advertisement or any other purpose.
